From a Garage Startup to a National Logistics Leader

Established in 2021 with a dream and a van, Pakisa Express Logistics has grown into a trusted courier partner delivering same day, township, chainstore and lastmile logistics solutions across South Africa.

What began as a single vehicle operation driven by commitment and reliability has evolved into a dynamic logistics network serving businesses nationwide. Our growth has been built on one principle: consistency in delivery and excellence in service.

Today, Pakisa Express Logistics operates through a strategically positioned warehouse network across South Africa. This allows us to provide seamless same day deliveries, early bird services, township coverage, chainstore distribution, and outlying area logistics support.

We understand that logistics is more than transportation — it is about trust, timing, and operational efficiency. Our systems, partnerships, and operational discipline ensure that every parcel is handled with professionalism and urgency.
