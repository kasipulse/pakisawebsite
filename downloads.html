How to Install & Enable

  • 1. Download: Tap the button above to download the APK file.
  • 2. Allow: If prompted, allow "Install from Unknown Sources" in your browser settings.
  • 3. Open: Install the app and open LethaScan from your app drawer.
  • 4. Enable: Go to Settings > System > Languages > On-screen keyboard and toggle LethaScan ON.
  • 5. Switch: Tap the keyboard icon while typing to switch to the LethaScan interface.
