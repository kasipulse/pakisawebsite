Proof of Delivery System

Pakisa offers a digital proof of delivery system that confirms when a parcel has been successfully delivered to the recipient. The system records delivery time, recipient details and delivery confirmation.

What is Proof of Delivery?

Proof of delivery (POD) is a confirmation that a parcel was delivered to the correct recipient. It may include the recipient name, signature, delivery time and location.

Digital POD Advantages

  • Instant delivery confirmation
  • Reduced disputes
  • Secure delivery records
  • Faster customer service resolution

Pakisa POD Technology

Pakisa records proof of delivery information directly in the system, making it easy for logistics teams and customers to verify deliveries at any time.
