Parcel Tracking System

Pakisa provides a real-time parcel tracking system designed for courier companies and logistics operations. Our platform allows businesses to track waybills, monitor delivery progress, and verify parcel movement across depots and drivers.

How Parcel Tracking Works

Each parcel is assigned a waybill number. As the parcel moves through the delivery network, scans are recorded at different stages such as collection, depot arrival, dispatch and final delivery.

Real Time Delivery Updates

Pakisa allows logistics teams and customers to view parcel status in real time. Updates include delivery progress, scanning location, and proof of delivery confirmation.

Benefits for Courier Companies

  • Real-time parcel visibility
  • Improved delivery accuracy
  • Reduced customer queries
  • Digital tracking history
